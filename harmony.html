Harmony Starter Plan

A gentle introduction to yoga for beginners seeking calm, balance, and mindful movement.

About This Plan

The Harmony Starter Plan is crafted for those beginning their yoga journey or returning after a pause. This program emphasizes slow, intentional movement that nurtures the mind and body. Through guided sessions, breath awareness, and foundational postures, you’ll build confidence, restore calm, and cultivate balance — both internally and physically.

Whether you're seeking stress relief, gentle mobility, or a peaceful daily routine, this plan sets the perfect foundation for long-term practice.

What’s Included

  • Beginner-friendly guided yoga sessions
  • Slow-paced foundational flow practices
  • Breath awareness & calming techniques
  • Basics of alignment and posture safety
  • Restorative stretching for gentle release
  • Short daily mindfulness exercises

Who This Plan Is For

  • Complete beginners exploring yoga for the first time
  • Anyone seeking gentle, peaceful body movement
  • People experiencing stress, tension, or overwhelm
  • Those wanting to improve flexibility gradually
  • Anyone building a healthy, calm daily routine

Benefits You’ll Experience

  • Improved balance, posture, and body awareness
  • Reduced stress and a calmer mental state
  • Gentle flexibility and improved mobility
  • Stronger connection between breath and body
  • A stable foundation for deeper yoga practices
